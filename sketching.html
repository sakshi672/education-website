SKETCHING

Sketching is defined as the creation of a visual representation of a composition in condensed form, which fully sets forth the melodic, harmonic and rhythmic structure of the composition, and does so in a manner whereby the Copyist may understand what was intended. Sketching is an additional service and the payment therefore shall be subject to individual negotiations between the Employer and the musician.
